About StudyOS

Your comprehensive learning management platform

Welcome to StudyOS

StudyOS is a comprehensive learning management system designed specifically for students at different stages of their academic journey. Whether you're in high school, preparing for competitive exams, or planning your post-10th academic path, we've got you covered.

Our Mission

We aim to provide personalized learning experiences tailored to your specific educational needs. Our platform helps you track progress, manage goals, and access curated resources all in one place.

Key Features

Personalized Dashboards

Custom interfaces based on your educational stage

Progress Tracking

Monitor your academic achievements

Goal Management

Set and track your learning objectives

Resource Library

Access curated study materials

Institution Support

Connect with teachers and classmates

Secure Platform

Your data is protected with modern security

Who We Serve

  • High School Students: Track board exam preparation
  • Exam Aspirants: Tools for JEE, NEET, SAT, and more
  • After 10th Students: Navigate your chosen stream

Technology

Built with Flask (Python), Firebase Authentication, and Firestore Database for a fast, secure, and reliable experience.
