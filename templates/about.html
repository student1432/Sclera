Welcome to Student Platform

Student Platform is a comprehensive learning management system designed specifically for students at different stages of their academic journey.

Our Mission

We aim to provide personalized learning experiences tailored to your specific educational needs, whether you're in high school, preparing for competitive exams, or planning your post-10th academic path.

Features

  • Personalized Dashboards: Custom interfaces based on your educational stage and goals
  • Task Management: Keep track of your assignments and to-do items
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your academic progress and achievements
  • Resource Library: Access curated study materials and resources
  • Secure Authentication: Your data is protected with Firebase security

Who We Serve

  • High School Students: Track your board exam preparation and overall academic progress
  • Exam Aspirants: Focused tools for JEE, NEET, SAT, and other competitive exam preparation
  • After 10th Students: Navigate your chosen stream with specialized resources and guidance

Technology

Built with modern web technologies including Flask (Python), Firebase Authentication, and Firestore Database to ensure a fast, secure, and reliable experience.
